📢 About the Event

Vociferates is a one-day creative showdown where branding brilliance meets strategic thinking.

  • 📅 Date: 16th April, 2025
  • ⏰ Time: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • 👥 Team Size: Max of 5 members
  • 🧠 Topics: Given on the spot — revolving around locations, patents, and more!
🎬 Event Rounds:
  • 🔹 Round 1
    • 🎥 Shoot and edit a promo video
    • 🎨 Design a poster based on the given topic
  • 🔹 Round 2
    • 💡 Pitch your branding idea to the panel
🎯 Rules:
  • 📸 Use your own camera and laptop
  • 🚫 No outside footage — all content must be created during the event
🖼️ Poster Design
  • Static digital poster
  • No AI-generated posters
🎬 Short Video
  • Minimum duration: 3 minutes
  • Location: Inside TCE campus
📜 Content Requirements
  • Must be original and created by the participant/team
  • Use the official TCE logo and Vyugam logo – unaltered
  • Must be suitable for sharing on social media platforms
🚫 Disqualification Grounds
  • Plagiarized or AI-generated content without originality
  • Inappropriate or insensitive content
  • Submission after deadline
  • Exceeding time limit
  • Irrelevant content
📊 Presentation Rules (Round 2)
  • Pitch Time: 7–10 minutes
  • Q&A with Judges: 2–3 minutes

Format:

  • Presentation must be in PPT, PDF, or Google Slides
  • Use visuals, mockups, and real examples where possible
🏆 Judging Criteria – Round 1 (100 pts)
  • Creativity and Innovation: 30 pts – Originality, fresh visual style, creative TCE branding
  • Clarity & Theme Relevance: 30 pts – Message clarity, relevance to promoting TCE
  • Social Media Reach: 20 pts – Potential engagement and virality
  • Time Management: 10 pts – Timely submission, good pacing and structure
  • Execution: 10 pts – No errors, clean formatting, good editing/transitions
🏆 Judging Criteria – Round 2 (100 pts)
  • Brand Positioning Clarity: 20 pts – Clear definition of brand identity and values
  • Marketing Strategy & Creativity: 20 pts – Innovative ideas and strong messaging
  • Design & Visual Appeal: 20 pts – Aesthetics, consistency, and quality
  • Practicality & Feasibility: 15 pts – Realistic, doable with available resources
  • Presentation Style & Delivery: 15 pts – Clear structure, confidence, time usage
  • Expected Impact & Measurability: 10 pts – Anticipated outcomes and metrics

📝 Registration Deadline: On or before 14th April
